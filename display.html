What Canada Displayed

Natural Resources: Canada exhibited massive white pine and maple timber, high-quality wheat, and luxurious fur products from the thriving fur trade.

Manufactured Goods: Visitors admired finely crafted furniture made from native woods, hand-built carriages and sleighs, and a selection of textiles and artisan crafts.

Special Highlight: A giant maple tree trunk stood out among the exhibits, symbolizing the vastness and richness of Canada's natural forests and impressing the international audience.
